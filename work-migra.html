MIGRA

Brand Identity


— A museum that changes and evolves every year to always offer new inspiration.


Description

MIGRA is the concept of a museum of graphics that is proposed as a dynamic place, which can allow visitors to see always different works renewing their collection continuously. The logo consists of three different versions, but created with the same guidelines. This, together with the coordinated image composed of diagonal lines and always different combinations of the three institutional colours, gives the brand dynamism and variability.
