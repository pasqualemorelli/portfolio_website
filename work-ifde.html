Il Fuoco dell'Eternità

Game Design


— Live the BAVA museum within a magical and extraordinary atmosphere.




Description

Il Fuoco dell'Eternità is an AR game set inside the Bagatti Valsecchi museum, in Milan. Thanks to the app and Aurasma, the user is involved in the narrative world designed around the experience so that he can experience his journey in the museum in a new and exciting way. It is based on the characters' voice that guides you in search of the hidden mysteries inside the museum.
